### **Universal Prompt Template for Image Generation**

---

**[Theme/Concept]:**

Describe the **general theme or concept** of the image in a single sentence. This helps the automation understand the context of the image.

*Example: “A peaceful evening wind-down” or “A relaxing study session.”*

**[Character/Subject]:**

Describe the **character(s) or subject(s)** in the image. Include their actions, emotions, and positioning.

*Example: “A person sitting in an armchair, relaxed and smiling, holding a book.”*

**[Setting/Environment]:**

Describe the **environment** or  **scene layout** . Specify the room, furniture, and other key details. Focus on minimalism, simplicity, and space.

*Example: “A cozy room with an armchair, a small table with a steaming cup of tea, and a few books scattered around.”*

**[Lighting & Color Palette]:**

Describe the **lighting** in the scene and the  **dominant color scheme** .

*Example: “Soft, warm light coming from a nearby lamp, creating a cozy atmosphere. The color palette is muted with pastel tones.”*

**[Background & Details]:**

Describe the **background elements** and **subtle details** that help enhance the mood of the image without overwhelming it.

*Example: “The background shows a faint city skyline at dusk through a window. A few indoor plants add a touch of nature.”*

**[Artistic Style]:**

Define the **art style** you want (e.g., hand-drawn, minimalist, vector style). This sets the overall aesthetic.

*Example: “The artwork should have hand-drawn lines, soft edges, and a sketch-like style with simple, clean shapes.”*

**[Mood/Emotion]:**

Describe the **mood** or **feeling** the image should evoke. This helps with the atmosphere of the scene.

*Example: “The image should convey a calm, peaceful, and tranquil atmosphere, suitable for a wind-down.”*

---

### **Example of a Full Prompt (using the template):**

**Theme/Concept:**

"A cozy evening wind-down."

**Character/Subject:**

"A person sitting in a comfortable armchair, wrapped in a soft blanket, holding a book and smiling."

**Setting/Environment:**

"The room is simple with minimal furniture: an armchair, a small wooden table with a steaming cup of tea, and a stack of books. The furniture is clean, unembellished, and placed for comfort."

**Lighting & Color Palette:**

"Soft, warm light from a lamp creates a calm ambiance. The color palette is muted, with pastel shades of beige, soft greens, and pale yellow hues."

**Background & Details:**

"Through the window, a faint city skyline can be seen at dusk, bathed in soft pastel colors. A couple of indoor plants and a small stack of books sit on the side table."

**Artistic Style:**

"The image should have clean, hand-drawn lines, with a sketch-like quality. Use simple shapes and soft shading to create a minimalist aesthetic."

**Mood/Emotion:**

"The atmosphere should be calming and tranquil, evoking a sense of peace and relaxation."

### **How This Template Works for Automation:**

* **Structured Elements:** Each section is a well-defined piece of the prompt. This allows an automation system to easily parse and adapt these sections to any subject or theme.
* **Flexibility:** The structure allows you to replace content (like themes, actions, colors, and emotions) easily, while keeping the overall prompt flow intact.
* **Consistency:** By using clear sections, this template ensures consistency across all generated prompts, making it reliable for repetitive use.

---

### **Quick Recap for Writing Prompts Automatically:**

1. **Theme/Concept:** A brief description of the scene.
2. **Character/Subject:** What is the character doing, and how do they appear?
3. **Setting/Environment:** Where is the scene taking place, and how is it arranged?
4. **Lighting & Color Palette:** How does the scene feel visually in terms of light and colors?
5. **Background & Details:** Subtle elements that enhance the atmosphere without overwhelming it.
6. **Artistic Style:** How should the art look (e.g., sketch-like, minimalist)?
7. **Mood/Emotion:** What emotion should the image convey (e.g., calm, energetic)?
